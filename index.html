About us

Loud Prints was founded and based in Bristol by a group of university friends who are passionate about streetwear, being content with current fashion trends but not overly excited. One day the group started painting on their jeans with fabric pens, then thought of a method of giving ordinary fashion aficionados the tools to create the garments of their dreams, starting off with a small pop-up where attendees would draw artworks and would compete with others for the shirt to be produced onto 30 tees, this was the humble beginnings of making Loud Prints. Since we have grown and worked with local charities, businesses and communities to let our beliefs and core values read and be clear in our vibrant designs.
